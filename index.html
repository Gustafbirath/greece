Acropolis & Parthenon

Step back in time at Athens' crown jewel. We'll explore the ancient citadel at sunrise to avoid crowds and capture the golden hour magic. The history and views are breathtaking.

🌅

Santorini Sunset

The world's most romantic sunset in Oia. We'll find the perfect spot to watch the sun dip into the Aegean Sea, painting the white buildings in shades of gold and pink.

🚤

Island Hopping

Private boat tour through the Cyclades islands. We'll discover hidden coves, swim in crystal-clear waters, and explore charming fishing villages accessible only by sea.

🏖️

Beach Days in Mykonos

Relaxing on the pristine beaches of Mykonos. From the vibrant beach clubs to secluded romantic spots, we'll find our perfect slice of paradise.

🎭

Ancient Theater Experience

Watch a performance at the ancient theater of Epidaurus, famous for its perfect acoustics. An unforgettable cultural experience under the Greek stars.

🛥️

Romantic Sailing

Private sailing trip around the Greek islands. We'll learn to sail together, enjoy fresh seafood onboard, and anchor in secluded bays for swimming and snorkeling.
